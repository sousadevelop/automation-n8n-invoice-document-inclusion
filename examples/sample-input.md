# Sample Input

This is a fictional example of the kind of input the workflow is designed to process.

## Gmail Message

- From: `supplier@example.com`
- Subject: `Invoice for May services`
- Attachment: `invoice-example.pdf`
- Attachment type: PDF or image document supported by the configured Gemini document analysis node.

## Attachment Content

The attachment should contain invoice-like information such as:

- Invoice date.
- Supplier name.
- Invoice number.
- Total amount.
- Currency.
- Taxes.
- Category or business context.
- Payment method, when available.

Do not use real invoices, customer data, private sender addresses, or financial records in examples committed to this repository.
